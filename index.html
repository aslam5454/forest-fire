Introduction on forest fires:

Forest fires is a very big problem .Due to forest fires wild animals suffer every year ,thousands of trees become ashes ,acres of lands lose their fertility and is of no use ,forest fires contribute to the air pollution and many more such problems . So preventing and reducing forest fires is crucial .But in these past years technology has played a big role in reducing forest fires so in this website we have done a data analysis on hoe technology has helped to prevent forest fires.

Our Work:

Our EVS project on prevention of forest fires explores how technology can contribute to reduce forest fire. We've researched remote sensing, fire weather forecasting systems,IOT sensors,AI and machine learning and preventive controlled burns , assessing their environmental impact. Additionally, we've analyzed their feasibility for widespread adoption, considering economic and technological factors. Our aim is to promote no forest fires for a greener future.
